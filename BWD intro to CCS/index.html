What is a graphic designer

A graphic designer is a creative professional who combines art and technology to communicate ideas through visual content. They play a vital role in various industries, including advertising, marketing, publishing, and web development. A well-designed piece not only looks aesthetically pleasing but also effectively conveys the intended message to the target audience.
