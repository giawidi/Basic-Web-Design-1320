What is a graphic designer

A graphic designer is a creative professional who combines art and technology to communicate ideas through visual content. They play a vital role in various industries, including advertising, marketing, publishing, and web development. By utilizing design software such as Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign, graphic designers create visually appealing designs for print and digital media.
